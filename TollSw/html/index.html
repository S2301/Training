About Us


Navayuga Infrastructure is a multi-dimensional entity with an extremely diversified portfolio. The company has earned a reputation as a pioneer in the industry, with unmatched domain knowledge and expertise in delivering projects on time and within budget. Our toll software is designed to make the process of collecting toll fees at the Toll Plaza quick and easy. With this software, user can make an entry of the vehicle and generate the toll amount for the vehicle, which is collected manually. The software supports a range of vehicle categories, including Bikes, LMV, HMV, and Heavy Trucks, with a fixed price for each vehicle.
